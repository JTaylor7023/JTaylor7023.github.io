Hennigans Whiskey was first brewed in 1932 in Up State New York. Hennigans Whiskey was first brewed by childhood friends, Colin O'Brien and Dylan Murphy. While details are unclear, the urban legend says that after getting sick of working in the peat fields, they jumped a steamboat to Sweden where, in the crisp clear Fjords of Gullmarn they discovered the "secret ingredient" that makes the whiskey oderless.
